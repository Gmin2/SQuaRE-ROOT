How the score works

CRQC Feasibility Score = clamp(20 × (1 − log10(V)), 0, 100), where V is the spacetime volume in megaqubit-days, V = (physical_qubits / 1e6) × wall_clock_days. It is a transparent proxy, not an endorsement of feasibility. Physical-qubit and wall-clock values use a documented fallback chain across report fields (hover a cell to see which source was used).
